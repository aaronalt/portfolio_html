Music Projects

k! / (n - k)!

  • January - 2021
  • Electronic, experimental, minimal

The track is made up of four different sequences simultaneously playing on a 64 note sequencer, to create a constant sound that is always changing yet never growing, and that starts and finishes with its core elements still intact.

Each of the four sequences have their specific purpose within the composition. An event modifier, a burst generator, and master clock are the main pillars of the structure.

Augusttremulous Lights [LP]

  • January - 2020
  • Post-Black Metal, Alt-Country, Dark Ambient

Although written and recorded while living in Southeastern Turkey, there are a few songs that remain as finished projects from time spent working in Alaska.

The theme stays consistent across borders as the environment for creation had changed: we are creatures that give our lives to a trade, but ultimately are left to feel washed out and out of touch with our own cultures.

Existential, solitary, and at times droning through the process.
